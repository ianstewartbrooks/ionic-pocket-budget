How to use

  • Click the at the top of the income and out goings pages to add a new item. This will open up a new screen where you can enter a name and amount for the item.
  • To delete an item swipe it right and click the bin icon
  • To edit an item swipe it left and click on the brush icon

Your Data

For your security and safety the information you enter into this app is stored on your device and not on some cloud server on the internet. The only people who will see the data are those who have access to your device.
